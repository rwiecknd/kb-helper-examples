Issue

How do I get my password reset?

Back to Top

Resolution

There are several options to changing or resetting your password. If you know your current or most recent password, and you have enrolled in Okta you can change it by visiting https://okta.nd.edu. If you are unable to change it yourself via that method, the following options are available:

Special Note: When you change your password, you will need to reauthenticate to all Notre Dame services, including Google Mail, Box, Eduroam, Office 365, CorpFS, etc. In some instances (e.g., 1Password) this will require re-typing your new password.

The timing of re-authentication is close to immediately. To avoid authentication issues during the workday that may interrupt your workflows, you may want to make the password change toward the end of your business hours.

Self Service

Once you've enrolled in Okta, you are automatically set up for self-service to reset your password. You'll find instructions for doing so here.

If you are unable to change your password yourself, contact the Service Desk. You will be required to provide information about yourself so that we can verify your identity.

Phone or Chat

  • Students: Students can call the Service Desk at (574) 631-8111 and supply the following information to receive a new password over the phone or via chat at servicenow.nd.edu:
    • Name
    • NetID
    • NDID (number)
    • Month and day of birth
    • Registered Listed Address or Phone Number
  • Faculty & Staff: Faculty, staff, and university affiliates can call the Service Desk at (574) 631-8111 and supply the following information to receive a new password via phone or chat at servicenow.nd.edu:
    • Name
    • NetID
    • NDID (number)
    • Month and day of birth
    • Last 4 digits of Social Security Number

    Alternatively, faculty and staff can get a new password by calling the Service Desk if identity can be verified by voice with another staff or faculty member at Notre Dame using a conference call.

    • Note: The Service Desk representative must be able to find the colleague in the ND Directory, get their extension from the directory, contact them at that number, and have them confirm identity to reset a password.

Video Conference call

If you have a computer or mobile device with video capabilities, call the Service Desk during normal business hours and request that they initiate a Zoom video conference with you. You can then show both your face and your photo ID to the camera to verify your identity. The Service Desk staff can then reset your password and provide it to you verbally.
