Question: How do I connect to the Eduroam wireless network?

Answer: To connect your device to the Eduroam wireless network or to ensure your device has the most recent and optimal configuration for the campus network:

  1. Connect to any wireless or wired network such as ND-guest. 
  2. Using a browser on the device, go to eduroam.nd.edu and follow the instructions to configure your device for the secure network.  
  3. Use this network for all official University business when you are on a campus wireless connection.
  4. If you are not immediately connected to Eduroam, reboot your device.

Note:  To connect to Eduroam, your device must be configured to use a wireless network connection.  Most laptops and mobile devices do.  For desktop computers, check with your manufacturer if your model has that capability.
